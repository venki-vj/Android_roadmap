❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this roadmap for?

Anyone wanting to learn Android development, from complete beginners to experienced developers looking to master modern patterns and system design.

Do I need prior programming experience?

The Beginner level assumes no Android experience but expects basic programming knowledge. We recommend pre-learning Python or JavaScript basics if needed.

How long will it take?

Beginner: 4-8 weeks, Intermediate: 8-16 weeks, Advanced: 12+ weeks. Timelines vary based on your pace and prior experience.

Is there a certificate?

No certificate is issued, but you'll build a portfolio of real projects that speaks louder than any certificate to employers.

Can I use this without internet?

Yes! The roadmap works offline. However, external links to documentation and videos require internet access.

How often is the roadmap updated?

We update quarterly with the latest Android releases, framework changes, and community feedback. Last updated: March 2026.

Can I contribute?

Absolutely! The project is open-source on GitHub. We welcome contributions, corrections, and suggestions.

Will this prepare me for interviews?

Yes! The Advanced level includes dedicated interview preparation materials and system design questions commonly asked at major tech companies.
